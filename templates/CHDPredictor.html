In order to predict the percentage of people with Coronary Heart Disease, we will define the following features and importance to be used for modeling training:

- PovertyRate - Share of the tract population living with income at or below the Federal poverty thresholds for family size (values 0-100) - Importance 1%

- MedianFamilyIncome - Tract median family income (numerical values) - Importance 21%

- lakidshalfshare - Share of tract population that are kids beyond 1/2 mile from supermarket (0-100) - Importance >1%

- laseniorshalfshare - Share of tract population that are seniors beyond 1/2 mile from supermarket(0-100) - Importance 18%

- TractSeniors - Total count of seniors age 65+ in tract (numerical value) - Importance 14%

- TractWhite - Total count of White population in tract (numerical value) - Importance >1%

- TractAsian - Total count of Asian population in tract (numerical value) - Importance 45%

- TractSNAP - Total count of housing units receiving SNAP benefits in tract (numerical value) - Importance >1%
